Sabre Wulf Walkthrough
This guide will help you find all four pieces of the Wulf Amulet, and it will lead you to all five Village Huts. Written instructions are just too confusing with this game, so I have supplied a map.

I do NOT own the original black and white map, although I have edited it showing the path you'll need to take.

The Red Path will be your primary path, starting from the "S". Along this path you'll also encounter Green Paths, which will lead to possible locations of the Amulet pieces. Since the placement of these pieces are random, you'll have to check all Green Paths. The screen that can contain the piece is the room with the Green Circle. Amulet Pieces also have a chance to spawn on the same screen as Village Huts. The pieces are not hard to spot; they're the only item that flashes yellow and blue rapidly. If a piece is not there, you can either rewind or backtrack until you're back on the Red Path.

Once you've found all four pieces, you can make your way to the "F" and complete the game.

-------
Notes:
-------

- Make good use of Orchids. Each of the five colors have a different effect, but all of the will make you temporarily invulnerable at the cost of temporarily losing your sword.
  • Red = Invulnerable, Slightly Slower Movement.
  • Blue = Invulnerable, Very Fast Movement (Best Orchid).
  • Purple = Invulnerable, Normal Movement, Inverted Controls (ex: Left D-Pad moves Right, etc.)
  • Yellow = Kills all destructible enemies on screen, Freezes player until they come back (Useless Orchid).
  • White = Returns player to normal if currently affected by another Orchid, if not affected by an Orchid than it does nothing.

- Use Orchids to get through random sleeping yellow Hippos and Natives, as they are invulnerable. You can hit the Hippos to make move, but this typically results in death with how close you need to be to hit them.

- Natives can come in the same colors as the Orchids, though the most dangerous has the Purple Native. Similar to any purple enemy, they will actively seek you out, but these guys will follow you for a couple of screens. Although you can hit a Native to change their direction, it sometimes does not have the desired effect.

- Keep moving. Staying on a screen for too long will cause a "Wildfire", and an invulnerable flame will move around like an enemy until you leave the screen.

- Beware the Wulf. At the bottom of the map, you'll see two very long stretches of road, the top one a bit smaller than the other. The Wulf prowls the top one. The Wulf is blue and completely invulnerable. Your only course of action is to run. Either hide in another path and wait for him to pass you, or try to use an Orchid to slip by him.

- On the very bottom path, there's also a pink Rhino. He's somewhat similar to the Wulf, but if you hit him with your sword he will change directions.

- This game is very much possible to beat without the Infinite Lives cheat, but you'll be rewinding A LOT as enemies can spawn directly on you with no time to react. The Natives can also pose a problem, as they can travel from screen to screen and can make some places impossible to get past without an Orchid. If you're just running through this game, use Infinite Lives.

------------------------
Legend:
Red = Primary Path
Green = Amulet Path
Blue = Village Hut
S = Start
F = Finish
